Please Read All Instructions Carefully

In this study you will classify noisy images with the assistance of an AI. Note that the AI is NOT 100% accurate. However, you should work with the AI to be as accurate as possible in your own decision. Your task is to do your best and be as accurate as possible. Some images will be easier to classify than others.

Below is an example of interface you will use to complete the task.

Trials & Images

There are a total of 128 trials in this study. Your progress is visible at the top of the interface (highlighted). You must complete all trials to be compensated.

Each trial you will be tasked with classifying a different noisy image (below trial counter). Note that some images will be easier to classify than others.

Making a Classification Selection

AI assistance is provided for each trial in the "AI Suggestions" box. The AI will assist you on each trial by eliminating (blacking out) certain options it believes are incorrect. To make your classification, you must click on one of the options. Select the best option available.

Click on the button to proceed to the next page.

Category Options

Each image you will classify belongs to 1 of 16 possible categories. All of the categories along with their text descriptions are displayed below.


Remember that not all category options will be displayed every trial. Therefore, you must click the single best option available to make your decision. You will only be allowed to make a single classification decision per image. However, you are allowed to change your selection before submitting your response.

AI Suggestions & Ordering

Each trial, the AI will assist you by presenting its top category predictions for the given image. You will be presented with either 2, 4, 8, or 16 category options. You must select the best option available to you. Below is an example of the AI presenting you with its top 4 prediction options for some image (not displayed).

The options displayed are the AI's top 4 predictions for some image (not displayed). However, there is no significance to the ordering of the options. The options are simply displayed in alphabetical order (from left to right). They are shown in rank order (most likely to least likely) based on the AI's prediction values. The most likely category (highest predicted class by the AI) is on the far left, labeled "1". As you move from left to right, the AI's prediction value for a given class decreases. The least likely category (lowest predicted class by the AI) is on the far right, labeled "16".

You will receive AI advice in this format for the entire study.
