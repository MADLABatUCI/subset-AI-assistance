Participant Information Sheet

Project Summary

We aim to study how humans use/interact with an Artificial Intelligence (AI) agent when making decisions. By having participants complete a task with the aid of an AI, we can learn: how humans integrate AI input into their decision making, how humans learn about an AI over time, what differences occur due to different presentations of AI aid.
